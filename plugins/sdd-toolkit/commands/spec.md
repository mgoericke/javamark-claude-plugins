# /spec

Create a domain specification for: $ARGUMENTS

Start with a short interview (max 3-5 questions) to understand the domain context:
1. Who are the actors?
2. What is the core process?
3. What business rules apply?

Then create the spec under `specs/` — purely domain-level, no technical details.

Afterwards, have the spec-reviewer agent review the spec.
